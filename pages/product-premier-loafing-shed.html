Premier Loafing Shed

Our Premier Loafing Shed is a versatile, multi-purpose building crafted with the same materials and expert craftsmanship as our Premier Series storage buildings. This adaptable structure can be customized to fit your specific needs, including as many stalls as required. A typical loafing shed layout includes one enclosed section, modeled after the Premier Series Tall Ranch or Tall Barn, along with one or more open-sided bays featuring dirt floors.

  • Ranch Style 4/12 Roof Pitch & 3-Tab Shingles
  • 6' 8" Clear Interior Sidewall Height
  • LP SilverTech Radiant Barrier Siding & 2x4 Wall Studs Spaced 16" On Center
  • 6' 2" Tall Shed Door Placed on Any Wall
  • 4" Boxed Eaves on All Walls
