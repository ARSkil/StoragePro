Premier Lean-To

Compact, stylish, and efficient—the Premier Lean-To is perfect for narrow side yards, patios, and spots where a traditional gable roof won’t fit. Its single-slope design sheds water away from adjacent structures and maximizes interior wall space.

  • Single-slope (lean-to) roof for tight-clearance installs
  • LP SmartSide® siding; 2×4 wall studs (typ.) on 16″–24″ centers
  • Durable shingle roof with ridge/edge ventilation options
  • Configurable door placement; optional windows and shelving
  • Engineered floor system for long-term stability
